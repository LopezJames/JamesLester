    ABOUT ME

    I am 19 years old; I live in Purok 20, Casisang, Malaybalay City, Bukidnon. I was born with a heart disease called Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA), which caused me many times to have seizures before. In the year 2021, at Northern Mindanao Medical Center, I was operated. Right now, I live happily since my seizure was not unlike in the past few years. However, there are times it triggers and still has a recovery today.

    MY SKILLS AND HOBBIES

    Playing Piano

    Playing the piano is one of my skills and hobbies; as I look at myself before, I am not a fan of music; I am more attracted to another category of art, which is drawings, paintings, and charcoal sketching. There was a summer before that for me was boring because there was nothing to do. Since our home has a keyboard, I practice how to play it until I enjoy playing it.

    Sketching/Drawing

    As I look at myself before drawing became my best friend when I was a kid. That skill and hobby sent me to the Special Program in the Arts (SPA) visual arts in junior high, where I discovered how beautiful art is.

    Listening Music

    As I discovered playing the piano, music became an inspiration that gave me the strength to do some work, like when I drew and many more. Music also aids me to reduce my stress and to relax when I am tired.

    Watching TV Series

    When I was in elementary, watching many series became part of my hobby, as I watched many anime series, which enhanced my imagination for doing such artistic things as drawing. Anime series show how beautiful the art of manga artists is and the plot written by writers. Similar to Korean dramas, which introduce the beautiful stories that thrill me to watch the next episode.

    EIGHT-YEARS FROM NOW

    As of now, as I am taking the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, I know that there are some obstacles in the front of my journey in college, but this is the stage where my skills will develop to achieve my dreams and goals, which is to become a successful person and become a software engineer eight-years later on. As of right now, I am learning about these concepts involved in software and topics about technology that will help me in my journey toward the destination I want. I realized that this path is not easy, but I believe my self-determination can be a key to success.
